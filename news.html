Ruber 0.0.1 released

2010-10-29

First public version of Ruber released today. It’s still alpha-quality and contains several bugs (most of them, unfortunately, appearing randomly and so quite difficult to fix). However, most of the bugs do not cause loss of data (at least if you enable autosaving), so you can try it.
